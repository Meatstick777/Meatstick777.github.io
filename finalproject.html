Final Project

These images explore the aesthetic potential of glitches, showing how corruption and distortion can create unexpected beauty. By embracing digital manipulation and experimental media techniques such as audacity, I highlight how even broken or altered visuals can produce striking effects. The more chaotic or "corrupt" the image, the more it challenges perspective, turning what might seem like errors into an expressive form of digital art.

This series of images captures the progression of glitches occurring on an arcade machine. I took this picture at a retro arcade store in San Jose, focusing on the video game "The King Of Fighters," which I particularly enjoy playing. Interestingly, when the last image was double-inverted, the characters on the screen became more visible, revealing hidden details within the glitches.

I took this picture of the sinking SS Palo Alto off the coast of Santa Cruz. It stood out to me because, even after years and storms, it remains partially intact, which reminded me of glitches and how sometimes broken or unexpected moments can create something visually interesting. The ship and its decay reflect both history and the beauty in imperfections.

Hellspawn, the comic character, is broken in both power and mind, constantly struggling with inner chaos and demons. This brokenness reminds me of glitches, where unexpected errors or distortions reveal something unique and striking. Just like glitches, Hellspawn's flaws make the story unpredictable and visually compelling.

Danny Brown's struggles with drug and alcohol addiction show how broken he once was, but now he is sober and creating music with clarity. Seeing him live at my first concert made the experience even more powerful, showing how overcoming brokenness can transform art. His journey is like a glitch, with unexpected messes and flaws that create moments that are raw, striking, and unforgettable.

I visited a hidden Japanese garden in Saratoga that felt calm, peaceful, and almost magical. Its quiet imperfections reminded me of glitches, where small errors and unexpected details create surprising beauty. The koi swam gracefully in the ponds, adding life and movement to the garden's captivating, secret charm.
