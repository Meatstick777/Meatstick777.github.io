Pacman Mouse Game

my game is inspired by Pac-Man and how the ghosts chase him around while he also eats them too. The game starts slowly but picks up pace once more ghosts are introduced which either help you in reaching the goal to win or make you lose points. The rules are to eat blue ghosts to gain points, avoid red ghosts because they deduct points, and reach 100 points to win.
